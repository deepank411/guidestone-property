5. Client Experience Journey (The Guidestone Method)

Our signature Guidestone Method™ ensures you make informed, confident
property decisions every step of the way.
Step 1: Strategy Call

 Personalized consultation to understand your goals, financial position, and
property aspirations.
 Discuss timelines, investment strategies, and risk profile.
Step 2: Retainer Payment
 Confirm engagement and secure your strategy session.
 Enables us to dedicate time, research, and resources to your property
journey.
Step 3: Market Research &amp; Targeting
 Comprehensive suburb and property analysis based on your goals.
 Insights on growth potential, rental yield, and lifestyle fit.
Step 4: Property Shortlisting
 Only properties matching your criteria are presented.
 Saves time and ensures strategic choices.
Step 5: Due Diligence &amp; Negotiation
 Review contracts, reports, and valuations.
 Handle negotiations to secure your property at the right price.
Step 6: Building &amp; Pest Reports + Handholding ✅
 Order professional building and pest inspections.
 Guide you through interpreting the reports and making informed decisions.
Step 7: Introduce Property Manager ✅
 Recommend trusted property managers for investors.
 Ensure smooth transition for tenants, rental setup, and management.
Step 8: Secure &amp; Settle
 Full support through settlement to ensure a smooth handover and peace of
mind.
Step 9: Post-Purchase Support
 Guidance on portfolio growth, refinancing, or further acquisitions.
 Continuous strategic advice for long-term wealth

Founder Story – Sonal, Founder of Guidestone Property
Property has always been more than just an investment to me — it’s a powerful tool
to create freedom, security, and long-term wealth.
My journey into property began as an investor, driven by the belief that property can be a
powerful tool to build long-term wealth and financial freedom. Rather than only learning
about property in theory, I chose to step into the market myself and began building my own
portfolio using strategies such as buy-and-hold, renovations, subdivisions, and cash flow
investing.

Through this hands-on experience, I realised that successful property investing is not about
luck — it’s about having the right strategy, selecting the right asset, and making confident
decisions at the right time.
Over the past three years in real estate, I have helped many clients secure investment
properties aligned with their long-term goals. Many of these clients have returned to build
further on their portfolios, which is a reflection of the strong assets we have secured together
and the trust built throughout the journey.
This experience inspired me to establish Guidestone Property — a buyers agency
dedicated to guiding clients toward smarter property decisions. As an active investor myself,
I assess every opportunity through both a professional and personal lens, focusing on long-
term fundamentals, growth potential, and strategies that can create lasting wealth.
At Guidestone Property, my goal is simple: to guide clients with clarity, confidence, and a
strategy-first approach to property investing.
